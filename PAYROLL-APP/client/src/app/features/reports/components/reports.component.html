SARS Submission Workflow

SARS does not offer a direct API for submissions. The standard workflow uses the e@syFile Employer CSV format.

Step 1: Generate Reports

Generate EMP201 (monthly) or EMP501 (annual reconciliation) from payroll data above.

Step 2: Export e@syFile CSV

Download the e@syFile CSV with SARS-specified columns.

Step 3: Upload to SARS

Import the CSV into SARS e@syFile Employer software.

Step 4: Confirm Submission

Verify submission status on eFiling.
